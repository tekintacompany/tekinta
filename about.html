Our Story

From a small startup to a leading tech solutions provider

Founded in 2018, TEKINTA Digital Solutions began with a simple mission: to provide businesses in Nigeria with reliable, innovative, and cost-effective technology solutions. What started as a small team of passionate tech enthusiasts has grown into a trusted partner for companies of all sizes across various industries.

Our journey has been marked by continuous growth, learning, and adaptation to the ever-evolving technology landscape. We've faced challenges, celebrated successes, and most importantly, built lasting relationships with our clients who trust us to deliver exceptional results.

Today, TEKINTA stands as a testament to what can be achieved with dedication, expertise, and a customer-centric approach. We remain committed to our founding principles while embracing innovation and excellence in everything we do.
