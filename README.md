## Project Overview

TEKINTA Digital Solutions is a leading technology company based in Nigeria, providing comprehensive digital solutions to businesses of all sizes. This website serves as the company's online presence, showcasing their services, portfolio, and allowing potential clients to get in touch.

The website is fully responsive, optimized for performance, and designed to convert visitors into leads through multiple contact channels including a contact form, WhatsApp integration, and direct calls.

## Features

- **Responsive Design**: Works seamlessly on desktop, tablet, and mobile devices
- **Modern UI/UX**: Clean, professional design with intuitive navigation
- **Service Pages**: Detailed information about all services offered
- **Portfolio Section**: Showcase of completed projects with filtering capabilities
- **Pricing Plans**: Transparent pricing with multiple tiers and bundle options
- **Contact Form**: Comprehensive form with validation and multiple submission options
- **WhatsApp Integration**: Direct chat functionality for quick communication
- **Blog Section**: SEO-friendly blog for content marketing
- **Performance Optimized**: Lazy loading, optimized images, and minified assets
- **Accessibility**: WCAG AA compliant with keyboard navigation and screen reader support
- **SEO Ready**: Meta tags, structured data, and sitemap for search engines
