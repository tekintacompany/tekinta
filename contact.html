TEKINTA offers a comprehensive range of technology services including web development, cybersecurity solutions, POS systems setup, system repair, software installation, virus removal, blockchain solutions, and technology training. We provide end-to-end technology solutions for businesses of all sizes.

Project timelines vary depending on complexity and scope. Simple websites typically take 2-4 weeks, while complex web applications may require 8-12 weeks.
